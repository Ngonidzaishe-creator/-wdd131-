Culture & Cuisine

Malagasy culture includes music (valiha), dance, crafts, and cuisine featuring rice, zebu, seafood, and tropical fruits. Traditional hospitality and strong family ties are central to daily life.

Tip: Try romazava (meat & greens) and mofo gasy (Malagasy pancakes) when visiting.

Wildlife & Conservation

Madagascar is famous for its endemic species â€” roughly 90% of wildlife is found nowhere else. Lemurs are the best-known group; conservation efforts protect habitats and rare species.

Ring-tailed lemur

Iconic species with a long, banded tail found in southern and southwestern Madagascar.

Aye-aye

Nocturnal lemur with long middle finger used for extracting grubs from wood.

Baobab

Ancient and massive trees, especially famous along Baobab Avenue near Morondava.
